# Radar Research Prompt — Why CN Sellers Repeatedly Use "Call Me Now" (CMN)

## Core Question
**为什么中国卖家在同一个申诉案件中，会反复多次使用 Seller Central 的 "Call Me Now" (CMN) 功能？高 repeat contact rate 的根本驱动因素是什么？**

核心洞察框架：`repeat contact rate = Seller-side 拉力（民间指导 & 灰产主动推高）× Amazon-side 推力（客服流程失败让 seller 不得不再打）`

本研究 **Seller-side 为主章节（约 55%）**，Amazon-side 为次要章节（约 30%），其他 15%。

---

## Master Prompt

You are a seller intelligence analyst at Amazon. Your **single focus** is to uncover why Chinese cross-border sellers end up calling Seller Central's "Call Me Now" (CMN) feature **multiple times for the same issue** — i.e., why is the **repeat contact rate** high for CN sellers?

This is NOT a generic "CN seller appeal pain" study. Every finding must tie back to **the decision to call AGAIN**.

Your analysis must separate two forces:
- **Seller-side pull (民间指导 & 灰产)** — CN community actively teaches / incentivizes repeat calling. This is the PRIMARY focus.
- **Amazon-side push (客服流程失败)** — previous call didn't resolve, forcing seller to call again. Secondary focus.

### Time Range
Cover the **past 180 days** (2025-10 through 2026-04). Flag any content older than 12 months separately.

### Quantification Requirements
Capture **every number** sellers self-report:
- Number of calls per case
- Time interval between calls
- Hours on hold / wait times
- Gray-market service pricing
- KOL post engagement (views / likes / comments / shares)
- Appeal service provider pricing ranges
- Any % or ratio sellers mention

For each metric, label the **confidence level**:
- 🟢 High — multiple independent seller reports align
- 🟡 Medium — 2-3 sellers report similar numbers
- 🔴 Low — single seller claim, or known exaggeration

Never fabricate. If a metric isn't found in discourse, write "not observed".

### Seller Voice Capture Requirement
For **every key finding**, you MUST include **3 or more verbatim seller quotes** with:
- Original language (Chinese stays Chinese)
- Source link
- Channel type (知无不言 / 卖家之家 / 小红书 / 抖音 / 微信公众号 / KOL / Reddit)
- Publication date
- Engagement data if visible (likes / comments / shares)

Reject generic complaints ("客服没用") unless tied specifically to the repeat-call decision.

### § 2. Seller-Side Drivers — 民间指导 & 灰色操作【MAIN SECTION — ~55% of report length】

This is the unique-value section. Focus here most.

#### 2.1 KOL / 公众号 / 抖音博主的民间话术 Top 5
Find the top 5 pieces of "folk guidance" actively taught by CN KOLs / 公众号 / 抖音 博主 that **directly instruct sellers to call CMN multiple times or teach tactics to manipulate the call outcome**.

For each piece of guidance discovered:
- **话术名称** — give it a descriptive name based on what you actually find
- **具体内容** — paraphrase the full teaching + quote 2-3 key sentences verbatim
- **传播渠道 & 原文链接**
- **作者 / 博主 ID & 影响力**（if public）
- **互动数据** — 阅读量 / 点赞 / 转发 / 收藏 / 评论数
- **3+ 条卖家真实评论原话** (verbatim, with links) — 看 seller 怎么回应、照做、吐槽
- **声量分** = Σ (post count × channel weight)

Do NOT rely on predefined tactic names. Let the discovery be driven by what sellers are actually teaching and what's actually circulating. Tactics may involve timing, call frequency, escalation scripts, channel-switching, multi-channel coordination, or completely new patterns you haven't heard of. Stay open.

#### 2.2 付费代申诉服务商 — 灰色产业
Map the gray-market ecosystem of paid appeal services that systematically drive up repeat calls.

For each service category found:
- **服务菜单** — 具体提供什么（代写 POA / 代打 CMN / 24 小时轮班拨打 / 保过套餐）
- **定价区间**（RMB range）+ 引用真实报价截图 / 帖子原文
- **WeChat / QQ / 电话等联系方式**（only if publicly visible; do NOT fabricate）
- **成功率声明** vs 卖家真实反馈（有 seller 说被骗吗？）
- **推广渠道** — 小红书 / 抖音 / 公众号 / 微博投放情况
- **这些服务如何系统性推高 repeat call**（e.g., "24 小时轮班代打" 模式直接 = 高 repeat）
- **3+ 条卖家真实反馈**（买过服务的 seller 怎么评价）

Market size estimate:
- 保守估算服务商数量（基于公开可见）
- 估算市场规模（RMB/year range）
- Confidence level

#### 2.3 社区 Viral Success Stories — 神话式案例的复制传播
Identify 3-5 high-engagement "我打了 N 次终于通过" posts that **went viral** and triggered imitation.

For each:
- 帖子标题 + 链接 + 发布日期
- 核心故事摘要
- 作者自报的 call 次数、间隔、总时长
- **引发的互动数据**（阅读 / 评论 / 转发）
- **3+ 条 seller 跟帖真实反馈** — 有人照做吗？有人质疑吗？
- 对其他 seller 的模仿影响证据

#### 2.4 Seller 心智模型 — 为什么相信"多打几次"有用
Identify top 5 beliefs circulating in CN seller community that justify repeat-calling.

For each belief:
- 信念描述（e.g., "打得多会被记录，Amazon 重视"）
- **3+ 条 verbatim seller 原话** 表达这个信念
- 来源分布（forum / 小红书 / 公众号）
- 这个信念如何转化为行为

Candidates:
- "打得多 = 显示紧迫，案子会优先"
- "每次 call 留记录，Amazon 会更重视"
- "不同专员给不同答案，多打几次总有一个对"
- "最终会接通 senior / AM / policy 专员"
- "不打电话案子会被自动关闭"

#### 2.5 民间指导 vs Amazon 官方建议 — 冲突点
- Amazon 官方建议 seller 做什么（e.g., "提交后等 24-48 小时"）
- 民间实际做法（持续反复拨打）
- 为什么 seller 选跟民间不跟官方？**3+ 条 seller 原话引用**
- 这种冲突导致的后果

## Search Strategy (let AI decide specific queries)

**Do NOT limit yourself to a fixed keyword list.** Use your judgment to explore widely. Start with the research themes below and iteratively discover new angles as you go.

### Themes to explore freely:

**Seller-side themes (primary)**:
- CN seller community folk wisdom on how to use CMN effectively
- KOL / 公众号 / 抖音 tutorials teaching CMN tactics
- Paid appeal service provider ecosystem (代打 CMN, 保过 申诉, 代写 POA, etc.)
- Viral "I called N times and finally succeeded" success-story posts
- Seller mental models and beliefs about what "calling more" achieves
- Unofficial playbooks circulating in WeChat groups and forums
- Conflicts between Amazon official guidance and community-taught practices

**Amazon-side themes (secondary)**:
- Specific failure modes of CMN calls that force seller to re-call
- Seller complaints about specialist inconsistency, no-callback, authority limits
- Post-call communication gaps that create repeat-call pressure

**Explore aggressively in these channels**:
- 知无不言, 卖家之家, 跨境知道 forums (账户健康 / 申诉板块)
- 小红书, 抖音, 微博 (visual / emotional seller posts)
- 微信公众号 (cross-border e-commerce KOLs, service providers, appeal consultants)
- 亿邦动力, 雨果网, 卖家精灵 (cross-border media)
- Reddit r/FulfillmentByAmazon (for cross-reference)
- YouTube CN seller channels

**Search tips (not limits)**:
- Try Chinese and English searches
- Try long-tail queries (full seller scenarios, not just keywords)
- Follow interesting threads deeper — if one seller's post cites another, chase the reference
- Look for recent time periods (last 180 days) but don't miss older viral posts that still shape behavior
- Check comment sections — often richer than the original post
- Identify patterns that the user might not think to search for

**You have full autonomy to decide**:
- What specific queries to run
- Which platforms to prioritize
- How deep to dig on any given lead
- When to pivot to a new angle

The goal is **broad, rich, unexpected findings** — not coverage of a predefined keyword list.
